Dear Hafshah

I feel like a complete failure—failed to make you happy, failed to make you feel truly loved. It feels sad, but at least I tried.

I know you're probably facing a lot right now: friendships, the future, maybe even your own inner struggles. But I believe you're strong and can get through it. Stay strong. If you ever feel lonely, I'm here—always supporting you, always wanting you to be happy and smile every moment, looking forward to all your accomplishments, and wanting to hear every story, whether it's about sadness, happiness, or hardship. I don't know who you love; maybe you love someone else. But whoever it is, if it can make you happy, then I hope you can be with that person.

Even though we feel more distant now and we're not together as often, that doesn't mean I've stopped caring. Honestly, this might be because I'm starting to feel like you don't need me anymore and are slowly pulling away. At first, I tried to deny it, but somehow, slowly, as I noticed your behavior, I felt like it might be true. I don't blame you for that, it's not your fault that you eventually started to get bored with me/our relationship. I think it's normal in a relationship when someone gets bored. And I realized that I couldn't force you to stay close/be with me because I thought it would really bother you, so because of that thought, I finally decided to slowly start to distance myself from you. Not because I wanted to, but because I was afraid that my presence would make you unhappy and that I would only become trash that would continue to bother you.

At that time, I felt that if I continued to force myself to stay by your side, it would only make me feel like trash. Honestly, I regret what happened between us, even if I were allowed, I wanted to continue talking and being with you forever. But that's impossible, right?.

See you when our fates meet again. I miss you too, love you. ♡

For you,

Mongi
